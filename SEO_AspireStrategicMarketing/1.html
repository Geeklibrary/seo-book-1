What is SEO?
Search engine optimization is the process of improving the
ranking
of your web pages on
search engines' organic or unpaid search results pages (such as on Google, Yahoo and Bing)
for search queries related to your business, brand, product or service.
Since Google is the leading search engine with 67% market share, an effective SEO
demands a deep understanding of its intricate algorithms and ranking factors. These are
continuously developing to display the most relevant search results to users. As the leading
search engine, it is critical to align with Google's best practices and this also means
exceeding the standards of Bing and Yahoo.
